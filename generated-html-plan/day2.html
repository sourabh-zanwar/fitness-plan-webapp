Day 2: Lower Body and Cardio

Time required: 1 hour 30 minutes

Warm-up:

Serial No. Exercise Sets and Reps Description
1 Light Cardio 5-10 minutes Engage in light aerobic activity to warm up your lower body muscles
2 Leg Swings 2 sets of 10 reps per leg Stand and swing one leg forward and backward, then switch to the other leg
3 Hip Circles 2 sets of 10 reps Rotate your hips in a circular motion, gradually increasing the range of motion
4 Dynamic Hamstring Stretch 2 sets of 10 reps Swing one leg forward and backward, focusing on stretching the hamstring muscles
5 Dynamic Hip Flexor Stretch 2 sets of 10 reps per leg Lunge forward with one leg, keeping the back leg straight and stretching the hip flexors

Lower Body Exercises:

Serial No. Exercise Sets and Reps Muscle Group Targeted Description
1 Barbell Squats 4 sets of 8-10 reps Quadriceps, Hamstrings, Glutes Place a barbell on your shoulders, squat down by bending your knees and hips, then return to standing position
2 Romanian Deadlifts 3 sets of 10-12 reps Hamstrings, Glutes, Lower Back Hold a barbell or dumbbells in front of thighs, bend at hips while keeping back straight, then return to starting position
3 Leg Press 3 sets of 10-12 reps Quadriceps, Glutes Sit in a leg press machine, push the platform away by extending your knees, then return to starting position
4 Calf Raises 3 sets of 15 reps Calves Stand on a raised surface with heels hanging off, raise heels by extending ankles as high as possible, then lower back down

Cardio:

Interval Training on Treadmill or Stationary Bike

  • 20 minutes total
  • Alternate between 1 minute of high intensity (sprinting or cycling at maximum effort) and 2 minutes of moderate intensity (jogging or cycling at a comfortable pace)

Cool-down:

Serial No. Exercise Sets and Reps Description
1 Light Cardio 5-10 minutes Engage in light aerobic activity to gradually lower heart rate and cool down muscles
2 Static Quadriceps Stretch Hold for 20-30 seconds per leg Stand and pull one foot towards your glutes, holding onto your ankle, to stretch the quadriceps
3 Static Hamstring Stretch Hold for 20-30 seconds per leg Sit on the floor with one leg extended, reach towards the toes of the extended leg to stretch the hamstring
4 Standing Calf Stretch Hold for 20-30 seconds per leg Step one foot back and press the heel into the ground to stretch the calf muscle
5 Seated Forward Fold Hold for 30 seconds Sit on the floor with legs extended, reach towards your toes to stretch the hamstrings and lower back
6 Downward Facing Dog Hold for 30 seconds Start on hands and knees, lift hips towards the ceiling, straighten arms and legs to form an inverted V shape
