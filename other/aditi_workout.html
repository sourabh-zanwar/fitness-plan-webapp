Day 1: Lower Body Focus

Warm-Up (10 minutes)

S.No. Exercise Sets x Reps Description
1 Brisk walking on treadmill 5 mins Increase heart rate and warm up lower body muscles
2 Dynamic stretches 5 mins Leg swings, hip circles, bodyweight squats

Strength Training

S.No. Exercise Sets x Reps Description
1 Barbell Squats 4x8-10 Keep chest up, core engaged, squat down as if sitting back into a chair
2 Romanian Deadlifts 3x10-12 Hinge at hips with straight back, lower bar towards floor until stretch in hamstrings, return up
3 Walking Lunges 3x12 steps Hold dumbbells, take large step forward, lower back knee towards floor, push back and repeat other leg

Cardio (20 minutes)

  • Combination of treadmill intervals and cycling, alternating between 1 minute of high-intensity effort and 2 minutes of moderate-intensity recovery.

Flexibility and Cool Down (10 minutes)

S.No. Exercise Duration Description
1 Static stretching 5 mins Hold each stretch for 20-30 seconds without bouncing
2 Stretching 5 mins Focus on quadriceps, hamstrings, calves, hip flexors

Day 2: Full Body Circuit

Warm-Up (10 minutes)

S.No. Exercise Duration Description
1 Light cardio 5 mins Jumping jacks, high knees
2 Dynamic stretches 5 mins Arm circles, torso twists, leg swings

Full Body Circuit

S.No. Exercise Sets x Reps Description
1 Kettlebell Swings 3x15 Hold kettlebell, hinge at hips, swing between legs, drive through hips
2 Push-Ups 3x10-12 Maintain straight line from head to heels, modify on knees if needed
3 Dumbbell Lunges with Bicep Curl 3x12 (each) Hold dumbbells, lunge with bicep curl simultaneously
4 Plank Rows 3x10-12 (each) Start in plank position, row dumbbell towards hip, alternate sides

Cardio Finisher (10 minutes)

  • 10 minutes of high-intensity interval training (HIIT) with 30 seconds all-out effort and 30 seconds active recovery.

Flexibility and Cool Down (10 minutes)

S.No. Exercise Duration Description
1 Stretching 5 mins Hold each stretch for 20-30 seconds to improve flexibility
2 Stretching 5 mins Focus on chest, shoulders, back, quadriceps, hamstrings, calves
