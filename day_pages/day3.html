Day 3: Full Body Strength and Flexibility

Time required: 1 hour 30 minutes

Warm-up:

Serial No. Exercise Sets and Reps Description
1 Light Cardio 5-10 minutes Engage in light aerobic activity to warm up your muscles
2 Arm Circles 2 sets of 10 reps Rotate your arms in small circles, gradually increasing the size
3 Leg Swings 2 sets of 10 reps per leg Stand and swing one leg forward and backward, then switch to the other leg
4 Dynamic Spinal Twist 2 sets of 10 reps per side Sit on the floor, rotate your torso from side to side, stretching your back
5 Dynamic Hip Circles 2 sets of 10 reps in each direction Stand and rotate your hips in a circular motion, alternating directions

Full Body Exercises:

Serial No. Exercise Sets and Reps Muscle Group Targeted Description
1 Deadlifts 4 sets of 8-10 reps Hamstrings, Glutes, Lower Back Stand with feet shoulder-width apart, bend at hips and knees to lower barbell to floor, then return to standing position
2 Dumbbell Chest Flyes 3 sets of 10-12 reps Chest Lie on a bench with dumbbells in hand, lower arms out to sides, then bring them back together
3 Wide Grip Lat Pulldowns 3 sets of 10-12 reps Back Use a lat pulldown machine with a wide grip to pull the bar down to chest level, then release back up
4 Arnold Press 3 sets of 8-10 reps Shoulders Sit on a bench with dumbbells in hand, start with palms facing you, press weights up and rotate palms away from you as you lift
5 Plank Rows 3 sets of 10-12 reps per arm Core, Back, Shoulders Start in a plank position with dumbbells in hands, row one dumbbell up towards hip while keeping core engaged

Cool-down:

Serial No. Exercise Sets and Reps Description
1 Light Cardio 5-10 minutes Engage in light aerobic activity to gradually lower heart rate and cool down muscles
2 Static Spinal Twist Hold for 20-30 seconds per side Sit on the floor, cross one leg over the other, twist your torso and hold the stretch
3 Butterfly Stretch Hold for 20-30 seconds Sit on the floor, bring the soles of your feet together, and gently press knees towards the floor
4 Cobra Stretch Hold for 20-30 seconds Lie on your stomach, place hands under shoulders, and push chest up while keeping hips on the ground
5 Pigeon Pose Hold for 30 seconds per side From a plank position, bring one knee forward towards the same-side wrist, then extend the other leg back and lower hips towards the floor
6 Full Body Stretch Hold each stretch for 20-30 seconds Perform a series of stretches targeting different muscle groups, including hamstrings, quadriceps, calves, shoulders, and chest

Flexibility and Mobility:

Yoga Flow or Pilates Routine: 20 minutes

Focus on stretches for the entire body
