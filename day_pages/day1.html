Day 1: Upper Body Focus

Time required: 1 hour 30 minutes

Warm-up:

Serial No. Exercise Sets and Reps Description
1 Light Cardio 5-10 minutes Get your heart rate up with a brisk walk, jog, or jumping jacks
2 Arm Circles 2 sets of 10 reps Rotate your arms in small circles, gradually increasing the size
3 Shoulder Rolls 2 sets of 10 reps Roll your shoulders forward and backward to loosen up
4 Dynamic Chest Stretch 2 sets of 10 reps Open and close arms, focusing on stretching the chest muscles
5 Dynamic Arm Swings 2 sets of 10 reps Swing arms forward and backward, gradually increasing range of motion

Upper Body Exercises:

Serial No. Exercise Sets and Reps Muscle Group Targeted Description
1 Bench Press 4 sets of 8-10 reps Chest Lie on a bench and lift the bar off the rack, lower it to your chest, and then push it back up
2 Pull-ups/Assisted Pull-ups 3 sets to failure or max reps Back Hang from a bar with palms facing away, pull body up until chin reaches above the bar
3 Seated Cable Rows 3 sets of 10-12 reps Back Sit at a cable row machine, grab the handle, and pull towards your torso while keeping your back straight
4 Dumbbell Shoulder Press 3 sets of 8-10 reps Shoulders Sit on a bench, hold dumbbells at shoulder height, and push them up overhead
5 Dumbbell Bicep Curls 3 sets of 10-12 reps Biceps Hold dumbbells at sides, curl them towards your shoulders while keeping elbows close to your body
6 Tricep Dips 3 sets to failure or max reps Triceps Use parallel bars, lower body by bending arms, then push back up

Core:

Serial No. Exercise Sets and Reps Description
1 Plank Hold for 60 seconds Keep body in a straight line from head to heels while resting on forearms and toes
2 Russian Twists 3 sets of 15 reps Sit on the floor, lean back slightly, and twist your torso from side to side while holding a weight or medicine ball
3 Hanging Leg Raises 3 sets of 10 reps Hang from a pull-up bar and raise legs until they're parallel to the floor, then lower them back down

Cool-down:

Serial No. Exercise Sets and Reps Description
1 Light Cardio 5-10 minutes Engage in light aerobic activity to gradually lower heart rate and cool down muscles
2 Static Chest Stretch Hold for 20-30 seconds Extend arms behind body, clasp hands together, and gently pull arms back to stretch chest
3 Static Triceps Stretch Hold for 20-30 seconds per arm Bring one arm overhead, bending at the elbow, and use the opposite hand to gently push on the elbow to stretch the triceps
4 Static Shoulder Stretch Hold for 20-30 seconds per arm Extend one arm across the body, use the other hand to gently press on the elbow to stretch theshoulder
5 Cat-Cow Stretch 2 sets of 10 reps Start on your hands and knees, arch your back up like a cat, then lower your back and lift your head like a cow
6 Child's Pose Hold for 30 seconds Sit back on your heels, lower your torso forward, and extend your arms in front of you with forehead resting on the ground
