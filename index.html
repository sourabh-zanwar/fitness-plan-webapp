Welcome!

Ready to improve your fitness? Use this tool to generate a workout for yourself!

To generate a workout plan for you, fill out the following form.

If you have already filled the form for generating workout, and you remember the unique identifier you submitted, you can find your workout at the following link https://sourabh-zanwar.github.io/fitness-plan-webapp/created_workouts/{your-unique-identifier}.html

For eg. for unique identifier 'abcdefg', you will find the workout at "https://sourabh-zanwar.github.io/fitness-plan-webapp/created_workouts/abcdefg.html"
