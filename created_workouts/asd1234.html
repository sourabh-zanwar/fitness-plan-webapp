Workout for Mudita

Day 1: Full Body Bodyweight Workout

Exercise Sets and Reps Targets
1 Squats 4 sets of 12-15 reps Quads, Glutes, Hamstrings
2 Push-ups 3 sets of 10-12 reps Chest, Shoulders, Triceps
3 Lunges 3 sets of 12 reps/leg Quads, Glutes, Hamstrings, Calves
4 Plank 3 sets of 30-45 seconds Core muscles
5 Glute Bridges 3 sets of 15 reps Glutes, Hamstrings

Day 2: Core and Cardio Bodyweight Workout

Exercise Sets and Reps Targets
1 Mountain Climbers 3 sets of 20 reps Core, Cardio
2 Bicycle Crunches 3 sets of 15 reps/side Rectus Abdominis, Obliques
3 High Knees 3 sets of 30 seconds Cardio, Lower Body
4 Russian Twists 3 sets of 20 reps Obliques, Core
5 Burpees 3 sets of 10 reps Full Body, Cardio
