Day 1: Full Body Strength & Cardio

Client: 27-year-old Female, 170cm tall, 63kg

Goal: Reduce glutes size, maintain good heart and lung health, achieve aesthetic body

Exercises

S. No. Exercise Muscle Group Sets x Reps
1 Squats Glutes, Quads, Hamstrings 4 x 12-15
2 Romanian Deadlifts Glutes, Hamstrings, Lower Back 3 x 10-12
3 Lunge Variations Glutes, Quads, Hamstrings 3 x 12/leg
4 Glute Bridges Glutes, Hamstrings 3 x 15

Cardio

  • 15 minutes of moderate intensity cardio (Stair Climbing or Elliptical Machine)

Warm-up and Cool-down

  • Warm-up: 5-10 minutes light cardio (jogging or cycling)
  • Cool-down: 5-10 minutes of stretching focusing on lower body (Hamstrings, Quads, Glutes)

Day 2: Upper Body Strength & Cardio

Client: 27-year-old Female, 170cm tall, 63kg

Goal: Reduce glutes size, maintain good heart and lung health, achieve aesthetic body

Exercises

S. No. Exercise Muscle Group Sets x Reps
1 Push-ups Chest, Shoulders, Triceps 3 x 12-15
2 Seated Rows Back 3 x 12
3 Dumbbell Chest Press Chest, Shoulders, Triceps 3 x 10-12
4 Bicep Curls Biceps 3 x 12

Cardio

  • 15 minutes of moderate intensity cardio (Rowing Machine or Cycling)

Warm-up and Cool-down

  • Warm-up: 5-10 minutes light cardio (rower or arm bike)
  • Cool-down: 5-10 minutes of stretching focusing on upper body (Chest, Shoulders, Arms)
