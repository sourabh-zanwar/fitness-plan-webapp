Workout Planner

Workout for Sourabh

Day 1: Full Body Strength

S.No. Exercise Muscle Group Sets and Reps
1 Squats Quadriceps, Glutes, Hamstrings 4 sets of 8-10 repetitions
2 Deadlifts Lower Back, Glutes, Hamstrings 3 sets of 6-8 repetitions
3 Bench Press Chest, Triceps, Shoulders 4 sets of 8-10 repetitions
4 Pull-ups or Lat Pulldowns Back, Biceps 3 sets of maximum reps or 4 sets of 8-10 repetitions
5 Dumbbell Shoulder Press Shoulders 3 sets of 10 repetitions

Warm-up: 8-10 minutes of dynamic stretching or light cardio

Cool Down: 5-10 minutes of stretching targeting major muscle groups

Day 2: Cardio & Core

S.No. Exercise Muscle Group Sets and Reps
1 Treadmill or Elliptical Cardio 20-30 minutes
2 Planks Core 3 sets of 30-60 seconds
3 Russian Twists Core 3 sets of 15-20 reps per side
4 Bicycle Crunches Core 3 sets of 15-20 reps per side

Warm-up: 8-10 minutes of dynamic stretching or light cardio

Cool Down: 5-10 minutes of stretching targeting core muscles

Day 3: Upper Body Strength

S.No. Exercise Muscle Group Sets and Reps
1 Barbell Rows Back, Biceps 4 sets of 8-10 repetitions
2 Dumbbell Chest Press Chest, Triceps, Shoulders 3 sets of 10 repetitions
3 Seated Cable Rows Back 3 sets of 10-12 repetitions
4 Dumbbell Bicep Curls Biceps 3 sets of 10 repetitions

Warm-up: 8-10 minutes of dynamic stretching or light cardio

Cool Down: 5-10 minutes of stretching targeting upper body muscles

Day 4: Lower Body Strength

S.No. Exercise Muscle Group Sets and Reps
1 Lunges Quadriceps, Glutes, Hamstrings 4 sets of 10 reps per leg
2 Leg Press Quadriceps, Glutes, Hamstrings 3 sets of 12 repetitions
3 Leg Curls Hamstrings 3 sets of 10-12 repetitions
4 Calf Raises Calves 3 sets of 15-20 repetitions

Warm-up: 8-10 minutes of dynamic stretching or light cardio

Cool Down: 5-10 minutes of stretching targeting lower body muscles
